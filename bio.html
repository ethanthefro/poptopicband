Pop Topic is Virginia’s premier MySpace Era Live Music Experience. Featuring a quintet of rock and metal veterans from the 757, they bring a high energy performance every time they hit the stage as they perform the music that shaped their alternative roots. Pop Topic’s mix of 90’s pop-punk, 2000’s emo classics, and everything in between will have you reminiscing of the times when setting your Top 8 friends was the hardest decision of the week.
